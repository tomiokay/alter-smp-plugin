## 1. Blade of the Fractured Stars

**Type:** Netherite Sword
**Theme:** Cosmic/Space/Stars

### Crafting Recipe (5×5 Legendary Altar)
```
NETHER_STAR    DIAMOND_BLOCK    DIAMOND_BLOCK    DIAMOND_BLOCK    NETHER_STAR
DIAMOND_BLOCK  CRYING_OBSIDIAN  AMETHYST_BLOCK   CRYING_OBSIDIAN  DIAMOND_BLOCK
DIAMOND_BLOCK  AMETHYST_BLOCK   NETHERITE_SWORD  AMETHYST_BLOCK   DIAMOND_BLOCK
DIAMOND_BLOCK  CRYING_OBSIDIAN  AMETHYST_BLOCK   CRYING_OBSIDIAN  DIAMOND_BLOCK
NETHER_STAR    DIAMOND_BLOCK    DIAMOND_BLOCK    DIAMOND_BLOCK    NETHER_STAR
```

### Passive: Flashburst Counter
- Count successful hits on enemies
- After **20 hits**, trigger Flashburst:
  - Find all entities within **5 blocks**
  - Apply **DARKNESS** effect (Amplifier: 255, Duration: 50 ticks / 2.5 seconds)
  - Apply **NAUSEA** effect (Duration: 100 ticks / 5 seconds)
  - Play enderman scream sound
  - Reset counter to 0
- Respects trust system (won't affect trusted players)

### Ability 1: Star Rift Slash
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
- Fires a long-range beam of energy
- Deals **12.0 damage** (6 hearts) to first entity hit
- Range: **30 blocks**
- Creates particle trail
- Respects trust system

### Ability 2: Stargate Blink
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
- Teleport to any location within your line of sight
- Range: **45 blocks**
- Creates particle effects at start and end
- No fall damage after teleport

**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
- Dash forward **8 blocks** in the direction you're looking
- Grant **Invisibility** for **4 seconds** (80 ticks)

## Trust System

All legendary weapon abilities and passives respect the **trust system**:
- Use `/trust <player>` to mark a player as trusted
- Your legendary abilities **will NOT affect** trusted players
- Normal Minecraft damage (sword hits, arrows) still works
- Trust is **one-directional** (not mutual unless both trust each other)

---

## Commands

### Player Commands
- `/ability <1|2>` - Use legendary weapon abilities
- `/trust <player>` - Toggle trust for another player

### OP Commands
- `/givealtar [player]` - Give a Legendary Altar
- `/giveweapon <weapon> [player]` - Give a legendary weapon
- `/givelegendary <legendary_id> [player]` - Give weapon for testing (doesn't mark as crafted)
- `/kreset` - Reset ALL legendary crafting
- `/kresetplayer <player>` - Reset crafting for specific player
- `/cooldown [player]` - Clear ability cooldowns
- `/lreload` - Reload configuration

## Crafting Notes

1. All legendaries are crafted at a **Legendary Altar** (5×5 crafting grid)
2. Each legendary can only be crafted **ONCE per server** (global limit)
3. Once someone crafts a legendary, no one else can craft it until reset
4. Recipes can be customized in `config.yml`
5. All damage values, cooldowns, and ranges are configurable
